Tamam

Helping You Turn Life's Transitions Into Purposeful New Beginnings

Welcome — I'm Heather Dolland Tamam, Founder of Doltam Creative Solutions, a certified MWBE consultancy dedicated to helping women reclaim their power, redefine success, and design lives they love.

I'm also the author of Create Your Own Table: A Guide to Discovering Your Purpose and Pivot: Because Life Doesn't Always Go As Planned , and the creator of transformational online programs including The Empowerment Blueprint, The Doltam Pivot Program, and Become a Content Creator.

Through my work, I guide professionals — especially women who feel undervalued in unfulfilling corporate roles — to rediscover their purpose and transform it into passion-driven businesses.

My Journey

My path has never been linear, and that's exactly what makes it powerful.

I began studying architecture, learning how to create strong foundations that withstand change. After years in corporate consulting, I realized that my blueprint wasn't meant for buildings, it was meant for people.

Like many of the clients I now serve, I once felt stuck between who I was and who I was becoming. Every pivot, every challenge, and every reinvention led me closer to my calling: helping others design their own version of freedom.

Today, I merge strategic business insight with mindset transformation to help my clients build lives that feel as good as they look on paper.

What I Believe

At Doltam Creative Solutions, every program I create reflects a simple truth:

Your story is your strategy.
Your experience is your expertise.
Your purpose already exists — you just need the right blueprint to activate it.

Whether you're ready to leave corporate life, launch a passion project, or transform your expertise into a business, my work meets you where you are and helps you create what's next — with clarity, confidence, and courage.

Thank You For Being Here

You're not here by coincidence. You're here because something inside you is whispering that it's time for more — more meaning, more freedom, more fulfillment.

I'm here to help you create it. Thank you for being on the journey.

Transforming lives through purposeful guidance and proven strategies.
